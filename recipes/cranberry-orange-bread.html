Cranberry Orange Bread

This cranberry orange bread recipe is fruity, festive, and wonderfully giftable.

Ingredients

    Batter

  • 2 cups flour
  • 3/4 cup white sugur
  • 1 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 3/4 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, cut into pieces and softened
  • 3/4 cup orange juice
  • 1 Tbsp orange zest
  • 1 large egg, beaten
  • 1 cup cranberries, chopped
  • 1/2 cup wallnuts, chopped (optional)

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (127 C). Grease an 8 1/2x4 1/2-inch loaf pan.
  2. Combine flour, sugar, baking powder, salt, and baking soda in a bowl. Stir butter into flour mixture until combined. Add orange juice, orange zest, and egg; mix well.
  3. Fold in cranberries and walnuts. Spoon batter into the prepared pan.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean, 60 to 75 minutes.
  5. Cool in the pan for 10 minutes before removing to cool comepletely on a wire rack.
